Bio

Edward is a Senior Research Associate at the University of Oxford and is known for developing decision-support models for both industry and government. He carried out one of the first assessments of 5G rollout in Britain. His work includes assessing the capacity, coverage and cost of new digital infrastructure, against the revenue opportunities new services can sustainably provide.
